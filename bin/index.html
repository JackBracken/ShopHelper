Mafia

Mafia (also known as Werewolf, Assassin or Witch Hunt) is a party game modelling a battle between an informed minority and an uninformed majority. Games can take whatever format the gamehost chooses, whether that of a classic mafia game with mafioso and townspeople, or with characters and roles from their favourite books or movies, such as a Harry Potter one with Death Eaters and villagers from Hogsmeade.
